# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Premium Residential Development Website

## 1. Project Overview
This project aims to develop a high-end, high-conversion landing page for a luxury real estate development located in the Tamboré region. The development is characterized by its "Resort Experience" and deep integration with nature, emphasizing exclusivity, security, and architectural excellence.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This document describes the requirements for the digital product (website) and the core features of the real estate project, without referencing the specific commercial name as per privacy and branding constraints.

---

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Primary:** Families with high purchasing power (Social Classes A/B+) seeking a better quality of life.
*   **Secondary:** Investors looking for high-appreciation potential in premium suburban areas.
*   **User Persona:** Professionals who value nature, security, and proximity to sophisticated urban infrastructure (high-end malls, international schools).

---

## 3. Product Goals
1.  **Lead Generation:** Maximize the number of qualified contacts via WhatsApp and integrated forms.
2.  **Brand Positioning:** Establish the project as a benchmark for luxury and ecological preservation.
3.  **Information Accessibility:** Provide clear details about unit typologies, amenities, and geographical advantages.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1. Navigation & Header
- **Dynamic Navbar:** Sticky header with links to Project, Amenities, Location, Floor Plans, and a direct CTA button.
- **Language Switch:** Support for Portuguese (Primary) and potentially English (Secondary).

### 4.2. Core Sections
| Section | Description | Key Elements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hero Section** | First impact for the user. | Auto-play cinematic video or high-res render; Value proposition headline; Primary CTA (WhatsApp). |
| **Project Details** | Architectural background and scale. | Names of renowned architectural, landscape, and interior design firms; Emphasis on low-density (6 towers in ~45k m²). |
| **Nature & Amenities** | The "Resort" feel. | Mention of 20,000+ m² of preserved green area; Internal hiking trails; High-performance fitness; Gourmet areas; Pet place. |
| **Typologies** | Floor plan exploration. | Interactive toggle for 106m², 136m², Garden units, and Penthouses (up to 277m²). |
| **Location** | Proximity to points of interest. | Distance/Time to Shopping Iguatemi, Shopping Tamboré, and major highways. |
| **Social Proof/FAQ** | Building trust. | FAQ section addressing common queries regarding security and delivery timelines. |

### 4.3. Lead Capture
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Floating pulsing button for immediate interaction.
- **Convertible Forms:** "Download Catalog" and "Schedule a Visit" forms with validation (Name, Email, WhatsApp).
- **Auto-responder:** Automated email/message delivery after form submission.

---

## 5. Design & Aesthetics (Look & Feel)
The design must evoke a **"Luxury Resort"** atmosphere.

- **Color Palette:** 
  - **Primary:** Deep Navy (#001F3F) or Charcoal (#1A1A1A) for backgrounds.
  - **Accents:** Gradient Gold/Bronze for CTAs and highlights.
  - **Neutral:** Warm ivory or light grey for text backgrounds.
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Garamond type) to convey traditional luxury.
  - **Body:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter or Montserrat) for maximum readability.
- **Micro-interactions:** Smooth scroll reveals, hover effects on unit cards, and subtle parallax for the nature-focused backgrounds.

---

## 6. Technical Requirements
- **Performance:** Initial load time under 2 seconds. Images must be served in WebP format.
- **Responsiveness:** 100% mobile-friendly. Real estate leads often originate from mobile social media ads.
- **SEO:** Optimized meta-tags, H1-H3 hierarchy, and Alt-text for all project renders.
- **Tracking:** Integration with Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics 4, and Facebook Pixel.

---

## 7. Project Constraints and Scope
- **Exclusion:** The site should not reveal the unit pricing directly without user registration (Lead Magnet).
- **Privacy:** Compliance with LGPD (Brazilian Data Protection Law) for all lead capture forms.

---

## 8. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of visitors who become leads via WhatsApp or Forms (Target: >3%).
- **Time on Page:** Average duration > 2.5 minutes.
- **Bounce Rate:** < 45% for organic traffic.
